The Hive houses a machine shop filled with power tools. Here, you can bring your woodworking projects to life! Simply bring your own materials, or use the scrap wood inside the shop. The power tools in the shop include miter saws, band saws and jigsaws to cut your material to the exact size you want it. There is also a sandblaster to make your parts smooth, rust-free and clean. The shop also has cordless drills, dremels, a drill press, belt sanders - and the list goes on. From a large piece of furniture to a small figurine, the machine shop has all the tools necessary to build whatever you can dream of.
